Guide

Phase 1 - Heal Kael'thas

General positioning:

  • Ranged and healers are roughly in the middle
  • Tanks and melee depends on what adds are up

Soul Pedistals

  • Non-healers can use the Soul Pedistals(markers) one person per pedistal at a time to convert health to healing. 2 min debuff after using -> Create rotation of people to use pedistal (stay as long as possible w/o dying).

Rockbound Vanquisher

  • Spawns in the middle of the room. Kill it before it gets too many casts of Concussive Smash off. Tanking swap on ~3 stacks of Vanquished (20 bleed).

Bleakwing Assassin

  • Spawns on the sides of the room (groups of 4-6).
  • Spread (8y) if you are targetted by Crimson Flurry.
  • When they reach 30% health they will cast Return to Stone healing to full after 8s (Hivemind esq). This is top prio for dps.

Pestering Fiend

  • Spawns around the middle (groups of ~8). Shattering Ruby does low physical damage to players and everyone 3y around them.
  • Interruptable but pointless, doesn't spell lock. Just loosely spread when these are up.
  • Typhoon etc to bunch them up as they don't spawn stacked. Explodes when they die (Heroic). Melee need to gtfo when they are close to dying

Soul Infuser

  • Top priority add.
  • Spawns in 2 small groups on either side of the entrance (groups of 1-3 in each). Either split 50/50 with even CC for the spawns or do one side at a time, try and see what works for us.
  • Not tankable. Will rush to the Pedistals and channel Soul Infusion damaging/healing (based on phase) Kael'thas.
  • CCable, Soul Infusion is also CCable (not interruptable).
  • When killed drops an Infuser's Orb. Clicking increases healing by 50% for 14s, stacking and refreshing duration. Single player use, biggest single target healer should stagger pick these to extend duration and effect to insane amounts.

High Torturer Darithos

  • Present at start, never respawns - low priority to kill
Mythic

Cloak of Flames

  • Occasional healing absorb on Kael'thas. 6 seconds to heal 2 million and interrupt, otherwise we wipe

Phase 2 - Kill Kael'thas @ 40% and 90%

Shade of Kael'thas

  • Spawns in the middle of the room with 60% first time, 10% second time
  • Despawns after losing 10% health, going back to P1
  • Adds from P1 remain, make sure we transition smoothly. Unsure if they keep spawning in P2
  • Remaining adds > Boss. If P1 adds spawn, find a nice balance, still wanting to get out of P2 ASAP

Fiery Strike

  • Tank ability, cone attack. Swap at 3 stacks
  • General P2 position: Active tank in front of boss, rest of raid loosely stacked behind boss

Shade of Kael'thas gains energy over time, at 100 energy he casts Blazing Surge

  • Large 40y frontal cone. Doesn't track the player (side steppable)
  • Beta notes: Listed to spawn fire patches but didn't. If this is the case, bait this close to a wall to save space

Reborn Phoenix

  • 2 spawns on either side of the Shade of Kael'thas
  • Fixates random players. Will just melee for some physical damage
  • Every 2.5 will do damage to everyone within 6y and leave a patch of fire
  • Ranged dps prio. CCable, keep in place to reduce fire on ground
  • When killed they slowly regenerate
  • Despawns when going back to P1 or fight is over

Ember Blast

  • A random player explodes after 5 seconds applying 6 stacks of Lingering Embers
  • Group soak to split both damage and stacks of Lingering Embers
  • Spawns fire patches when detonating, place it at the edge / low traffic area

Fire patches

  • They will remain going into P1 for approx 1 min
Mythic

Cloak of Flames

  • Occasional damage absorb on Shade of Kael'thas, top priority
  • Might have to assign dps cooldown if we struggle with regular dps switching

Reborn Phoenix

  • Doesn't despawn when going back to P1
